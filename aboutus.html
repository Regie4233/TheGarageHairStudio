The Garage Hair Studio

Stacy

I founded The Garage Hair Studio in 2013 with a vision to create a professional, comfortable space for men to get their haircut.
I started in the industry 27yrs ago. My training began in a unisex salon and I quickly knew that I enjoyed doing short haircuts for both men and women.
I excel in scissor and razor cuts, as well as clipper cuts. Other than taking haircutting advanced classes over the years, I have also studied color theory and technique.
If you can’t find me in the shop then I am sure to be hanging with my fury brood of 2 dogs and 2 cats, as I am an avid animal lover.
